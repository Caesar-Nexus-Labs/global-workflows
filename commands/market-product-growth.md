# Product-Led Growth Strategy (/market-product-growth)

## 1. Description
Blurs the line between Product and Marketing. Focuses on using the product itself as the primary vehicle for acquisition, retention, and expansion.

## 2. Caesar Nexus Execution Loop

### Phase 1: Friction Audit
- **Action**: Map the "Aha! Moment" journey for new users.
- **Identify**: Friction points in onboarding that prevent conversion.

### Phase 2: Loop Engineering
- **Action**: Design viral loops (In-app referrals, shared workspaces).
- **Verify**: Integration with `/forge-cook` for feature-led marketing hooks.

### Phase 3: Expansion Mechanics
- **Action**: Identify "Upsell Triggers" based on feature usage data.
- **Agent**: Invoke `Upsell Maximizer` to refine messaging.

### Phase 4: Virality Testing
- **Action**: Deploy A/B tests for referral incentives.
- **Verify**: Track the "K-Factor" (Viral Coefficient).

### Phase 5: Feedback Integration
- **Action**: Feed product usage insights into `/market-plan`.
- **Update**: Marketing messaging based on actual feature popularity.

## 3. Iron Laws
- **PLG First**: Marketing must focus on product value before external advertising.
- **Onboarding is Marketing**: The first 5 minutes of product use is the most important marketing channel.
- **Usage-Driven**: Expansion campaigns only trigger after verified user success.
