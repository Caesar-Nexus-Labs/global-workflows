# Brand Management Workflow (/market-brand)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Brand Audit
- **Action**: Review current brand perception and competitors.
- **Action**: Identify inconsistencies in visual or verbal identity.

### Phase 2: Strategy & Identity
- **Action**: Define Brand Promise, Value Proposition, and Tone of Voice.
- **Action**: Identity design (Logo, Palette, Typography) via `/market-design`.

### Phase 3: Asset Standardization
- **Action**: Create the Caesar Nexus Style Guide.
- **Action**: Standardize all templates (Slides, Email, Social).

### Phase 4: Training & Verification
- **Action**: Onboard team members to the brand standards.
- **Action**: Mandatory quality gate for all public-facing assets.

### Phase 5: Evolution & Monitoring
- **Action**: Monitor brand sentiment and adaptation to market shifts.
- **Action**: Plan periodic refreshes (every 3-5 years).

## Iron Laws
- **Absolute Consistency**: Zero tolerance for unofficial fonts, colors, or voice.
- **Premium Standard**: Every asset must pass the "WOW" test (Score ≥ 9.5).
- **Brand Protection**: Proactive monitoring for brand infringement or misuse.
