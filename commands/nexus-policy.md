# Policy Management (/nexus-policy)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Policy Research
- **Action**: Benchmark industry standards and legal requirements.
- **Goal**: Define internal "Iron Laws" for target domains.

### Phase 2: Design & Blueprinting
- **Action**: Craft policy language and technical enforcement rules.
- **Verify**: 100% English and Caesar Nexus branding.

### Phase 3: Implementation (Locking)
- **Implement**: Inject policies into `*.rules.md` or CI/CD gates.
- **Verify**: Policy visibility to all agents.

### Phase 4: Audit & Review
- **Call**: `/nexus-audit`
- **Action**: Verify policy effectiveness and team adherence.

### Phase 5: Distribution
- **Action**: Update Compliance Kit Master Index.
- **Update**: Team notifications on policy changes.

## Iron Laws
- **Clarity First**: Policies must be unambiguous and actionable.
- **Strict Adherence**: Zero-bypass policy for critical security/brand rules.
- **Living Document**: Policies must evolve with market and tech changes.
