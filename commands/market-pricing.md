# Pricing Strategy Workflow (/market-pricing)

## 1. What This Workflow Does
Psychological and data-driven pricing optimization. Maximizes Revenue Per User (RPU).

## 2. Actions
- **Tier Design**: Free vs Pro vs Enterprise boundaries.
- **Price Sensitivity**: Analyzes conversion drop-off at different price points.
- **Anchor Pricing**: Designing the "Decoy" plan to push users toward the middle tier.
