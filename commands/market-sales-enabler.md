# Sales Enablement Workflow (/market-sales)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Prospect Identification
- **Action**: Lead qualification and scoring via `/market-lead-qualifier`.
- **Goal**: Identify "Sale-Ready" opportunities.

### Phase 2: Material Blueprinting
- **Action**: Identify gaps in the current sales deck or collateral.
- **Action**: Plan custom proposals or ROI calculators.

### Phase 3: Execution (Material Creation)
- **Call**: `/market-write`, `/market-design`.
- **Implement**: Pitch decks, Product Guides, and Comparison Charts.

### Phase 4: Training & Alignment
- **Action**: Coordinate with the sales team for objection handling and product knowledge.
- **Action**: Update the Sales Playbook.

### Phase 5: Post-Sale & Feedback
- **Action**: Customer success handoff and feedback collection.
- **Action**: Feed sales insights back into the Marketing Strategy via `/market-plan`.

## Iron Laws
- **Relevance**: All materials must be tailored to the specific vertical or persona.
- **Atomic Updates**: Sales assets must be updated within 24 hours of a product change.
- **Zero Friction**: Materials must be easily accessible and shareable.
