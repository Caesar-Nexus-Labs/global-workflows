# Strategic Pattern Extraction (/market-extract-pattern)

## 1. Description
The "Distillation" engine of Caesar Nexus Marketing. It extracts high-value strategic patterns, messaging frameworks, and growth loops from successful market leaders to create optimized, high-performance clones or derivatives.

## 2. Caesar Nexus Execution Loop

### Phase 1: Pattern Identification (Scout)
- **Action**: Audit competitor growth loops (Referral, Virality, Retention).
- **Invoke**: `/market-product-growth` insights.

### Phase 2: Strategic Distillation (Blueprint)
- **Action**: Extract the core messaging "Hooks" and "Value Stacks".
- **Agent**: `Pattern Extractor Agent` execution.

### Phase 3: DNA Synthesis (Cook)
- **Action**: Synthesis of a new Marketing Framework based on distilled patterns.
- **Call**: `/market-write` to generate de-branded, high-conversion copy.

### Phase 4: Integrity Check (Gate)
- **Action**: Ensure the new pattern is "Caesar-Nexus" aligned and optimized for the current tech stack.
- **Verify**: Check for "Wow" factor and premium aesthetics.

### Phase 5: Handoff to Campaign (Watch)
- **Action**: Integrate the distilled pattern into active campaigns.
- **Call**: `/market-campaign` for immediate execution.

## 3. Iron Laws
- **High-Fidelity Only**: Extracted patterns must be ≥ 95% aligned with demonstrated market success.
- **Zero Trace**: Absolutely no reference to the source of inspiration in public-facing assets.
- **Dynamic Adaptation**: Patterns must be adapted for Caesar Nexus unique value proposition, not just copied.
