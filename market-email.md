# Email Marketing Workflow (/market-email)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Segmentation & Goals
- **Action**: Audience selection and behavioral segmentation.
- **Goal**: Define specific goal (Welcome, Nurture, Promo).

### Phase 2: Copy & Design
- **Action**: Subject line brainstorming and body copy creation.
- **Action**: Template design following the Caesar Nexus premium aesthetic.

### Phase 3: A/B Test Setup
- **Action**: Set up tests for subject lines, send times, or CTA placement.
- **Verify**: Preview testing across all major email clients.

### Phase 4: Execution (Scheduling)
- **Action**: Automation flow setup and final scheduling.
- **Agent**: `email-manager` audit of deliverability.

### Phase 5: Monitoring & Analysis
- **Call**: `/market-analytics`
- **Action**: Real-time monitoring of opens, clicks, and unsubscribes.

## Iron Laws
- **Compliance**: Zero tolerance for SPAM; 100% adherence to CAN-SPAM/GDPR.
- **Clean Lists**: Immediate removal of bounces and inactive subscribers.
- **Mobile First**: All templates must be perfectly responsive.
