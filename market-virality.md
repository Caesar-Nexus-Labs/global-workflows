# Virality & Referrals Workflow (/market-virality)

## 1. What This Workflow Does
Social proof and exponential growth mechanics. Designing "Loops" that encourage users to bring more users.

## 2. Actions
- **Referral Loop**: Double-sided incentives (Invite a friend, get a month free).
- **Product Loops**: Shared artifacts (e.g. "Made with Caesar Nexus" badges).
- **Network Effects**: Features that become more valuable as more teammates join.
