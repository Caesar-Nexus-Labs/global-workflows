# Sales Enablement Workflow (/market-sales)

## 1. What This Workflow Does
Collateral and pitch optimization. Provides the sales team (or AI sales agents) with the exact scripts and assets needed to close a qualified lead.

## 2. Actions
- **Script Generation**: Creates personalized outreach based on the lead's persona.
- **Pitch Deck Optimization**: Tailors visual assets to the specific pain points identified by the Lead Qualifier.
- **Objection Handling**: Pre-computes responses to common sales hurdles.
