# SEO Workflow (/market-seo)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Audit & Foundation
- **Action**: Execute technical SEO audit and keyword research.
- **Action**: Benchmark competitors and identify content gaps via `/market-research`.

### Phase 2: Planning & Targeting
- **Call**: `/market-plan`
- **Action**: Define keyword strategy and internal linking architecture.

### Phase 3: Execution (Optimization)
- **Call**: `/market-content`
- **Action**: Implement on-page optimization (Meta tags, H-tags, Image ALT).
- **Action**: Manage off-page backlink and authority building.

### Phase 4: Continuous Monitoring
- **Call**: `/market-analytics`
- **Action**: Track rankings and organic traffic growth.
- **Action**: Audit technical performance (Lighthouse/Core Web Vitals).

### Phase 5: Adaptive Refinement
- **Action**: Update underperforming content and expand winning topics.
- **Action**: Adapt strategy to algorithm updates and market shifts.

## Iron Laws
- **White Hat Only**: Zero-tolerance for black-hat tactics; prioritize long-term authority.
- **Quality Over Quantity**: One "Mega-post" is better than ten thin pages.
- **Performance Gated**: SEO success requires PageSpeed score ≥ 90.
