# Content Marketing Workflow (/market-content)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Planning & Research
- **Action**: Content calendar audit and topic research via `/market-research`.
- **Goal**: Identify keyword targeting and audience "pain points".

### Phase 2: Blueprinting
- **Action**: Create detailed outlines for Blog/Video/Guide assets.
- **Action**: Define visual style and media requirements.

### Phase 3: Execution (Creation)
- **Action**: Writing via AI assistants, editing, and refinement.
- **Implement**: Visual design and media production via `/market-design`.

### Phase 4: Optimization
- **Call**: `/market-seo`
- **Action**: On-page optimization, metadata, and internal linking audit.

### Phase 5: Distribution & Finalization
- **Action**: Publishing to CMS and promotions via `/market-social`.
- **Verify**: Cross-channel performance tracking.

## Iron Laws
- **Value First**: Content must provide genuine utility before promotion.
- **Atomic Repurposing**: Every major asset (Blog) must be repurposed into at least 3 social snippets.
- **SEO Gated**: No "Raw" content; everything must be SEO-optimized before publishing.
