# Social Media Workflow (/market-social)

## Caesar Nexus Execution Logic

### Phase 1: Review & Listen
- **Action**: Review overnight engagement and respond to comments/DMs.
- **Action**: Monitor trending topics and industry news for participation opportunities.

### Phase 2: Design & Scheduling
- **Action**: Create posts following the daily content mix (Educational: 50%, Promo: 20%, Engagement: 30%).
- **Action**: Schedule across platforms (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok).

### Phase 3: Engagement & Interaction
- **Action**: Real-time interaction with the audience to build community.
- **Action**: Collaborative engagement with partners and industry leaders.

### Phase 4: Verification
- **Action**: Verify post formatting and media quality across all devices.
- **Action**: Ensure alignment with current campaign messaging.

### Phase 5: Monitoring & Analysis
- **Action**: Analyze day's metrics (Followers, Reach, Engagement Rate).
- **Action**: Feed insights into tomorrow's content planning.

## Iron Laws
- **The 80/20 Rule**: 80% value/education, 20% direct promotion.
- **Engagement Threshold**: Mandatory response to all genuine comments within 4 hours.
- **Brand Voice**: Strictly adhere to the established Caesar Nexus tone of voice.
